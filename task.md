Official Portfolio MVP 
Your portfolio site is an extension of you. It needs to have the following


About Section
Dev Description: Your Name, and Bio - Feel free to add a professional image of yourself if you have one, if not I can take one of you!
Projects Section
Carousel Display of Completed Projects - Images/screenshots of your application. Cards that when clicked on lead to a live web page. Icons in the description of the project that lead to the git hub repo.
Skills
Component laying out your known languages/skills
Use any of the inspiration portfolio sites to display these skills in a unique way.
Contact
Form for people to contact you. Users should be able to fill out a subject and message and send an email directly to you.
PDF Resume Download
This can be present in the contact area or a tab in the nav bar (anywhere works)
